What is this?

You know how after a race you think "I should really write down what happened so I don't do that again"? And then you don't, and three races later you're in the wall at the same corner? Yeah, us too.

Saturday's Spinout syncs with your iRacing account to pull in your race history, then lets you add notes about what went well, what didn't, and whether that dive-bomb was actually your fault (it probably was).

Automatic Sync

Your races are pulled from iRacing. No manual entry needed.

Race Journal

Add notes and track your sentiment for each session.

Track Your Progress

See your iRating and Safety Rating changes over time.

Works on Mobile

Browse your race history from your phone.

Analytics

Slice your stats by series, car, or track. Finally confirm what you've always suspected about that one oval.
